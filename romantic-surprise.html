For You, Val

a story written in heartbeats

01

the first moment

I don't remember exactly when it happened. Maybe it was the first time you laughed at something stupid I said. Maybe it was the way you looked at me like I actually mattered. All I know is that somewhere between hello and now, you became the most important person in my world.

You walked into my life like you'd always been there. Quietly. Gently. And suddenly, everything felt different. Better. Brighter.

02

the little things

It's not one big thing. It's a thousand small ones. The way you get excited about the little joys. The way you care without expecting anything back. The way your eyes light up when you talk about something you love.

I notice everything about you. The way you tilt your head when you're thinking. The way your voice softens when you're tired. The way you exist — beautifully, gently, without even trying.

03

a letter for you

"I've rewritten this so many times. Not because I don't know what to say — but because words feel too small for what you mean to me."
